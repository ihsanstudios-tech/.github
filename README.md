## Active Development: The Productive Muslim (TPM)

Our flagship project is a sophisticated scheduling engine designed to solve the challenge of "Salah Fragmentation." Unlike traditional calendar applications, TPM utilizes a deterministic approach to time management.

* **Hard Constraint Logic:** The scheduling engine treats the five daily prayers as immovable anchors, dynamically optimizing all other tasks—such as lectures, meetings, and study sessions—around these windows.
* **Iqamah Synchronization:** Integration with local Masjid data to ensure users are synchronized with their specific community congregation times.
* **Deep Work Protection:** Algorithmic identification of focus windows to ensure academic and professional excellence is maintained through structured time-blocking.
